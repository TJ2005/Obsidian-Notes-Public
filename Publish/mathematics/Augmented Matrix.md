# Augmented Matrix


Note that the matrix
$$[ \begin{bmatrix}
    a_{11} & a_{12} & \ldots & a_{1n} & b_1 \\
    a_{21} & a_{22} & \ldots & a_{2n} & b_2 \\
    \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\
    a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \ldots & a_{mn} & b_m
\end{bmatrix} 
is\ called\ the \ augmented \ matrix. It\  is\  represented\ as\  ([A | B]).$$
